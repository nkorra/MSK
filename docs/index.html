About MSK

Engineering-led manufacturing from Hyderabad.

MSK Precision Engineering Group supports industrial customers with design review, precision manufacturing, analysis planning and inspection-focused execution. We work where drawings, tolerances, loading and delivery discipline matter.

Core Capabilities

Precision support in six focused areas.

CNC Manufacturing

Turning, milling and production support for precision parts.

CAD / CAM / CAE

Model review, drafting and manufacturability preparation.

FEA / CFD

Analysis planning, assumptions and result review support.

3D Printing

Additive prototypes, fixtures and low-volume parts.

Inspection

Dimensional review, finding records and technical evidence.

Training

Practical CNC, CAD/CAM and engineering fundamentals.

Industries

Industries We Serve

Defence

Precision components, brackets, housings, fixtures and prototypes for defence platforms and test rigs.

Aerospace

High-tolerance parts and assemblies for flight-support equipment, ground systems and test hardware.

Oil & Gas

Components for topside packages, skids, handling systems and critical support equipment.

Marine & Offshore

Corrosion-resistant fittings, structural mounts and retrofit parts for ships, offshore and port assets.

Robotics & Automation

End-effectors, sensor brackets, frames and custom mechanical interfaces for robotic systems.

Automotive & EV

Prototype tooling, EV fixtures and performance components for modern mobility applications.

Rail

Bogie parts, couplers, braking components, signalling hardware and legacy spares.

Agriculture

Wear-resistant components for equipment, implements, automation and irrigation systems.

Training

Training & Development

Focused technical training for engineers, technicians and manufacturing teams.

CNC Programming

Turning, milling, multi-axis strategies, G/M codes, tooling and setup best practices.

CAD/CAM Design

Solid modeling, simulation, post-processing and production-ready handoffs.

Additive Manufacturing

Design for AM, build orientation, supports, post-processing and finishing.

Structural & Fluid Analysis

FEA and CFD fundamentals with reporting suitable for audits and design reviews.
