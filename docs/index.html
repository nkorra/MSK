About MSK – Our Story

MSK Precision Engineering Group was founded in Hyderabad with a clear mission: to deliver precision, reliability and engineering-driven solutions for advanced industries.

Starting as a small workshop in Cherlapally, MSK has grown into a manufacturing and engineering partner for Defence, Aerospace, Oil & Gas, Marine & Offshore, Automotive, EV, Robotics and Rail sectors. Our team blends mechanical engineering, naval architecture, FEA/CFD analysis, CNC machining and digital manufacturing to help clients reduce lead times, improve quality and accelerate product development.

Today, MSK is building a new facility at IP-Madharam to support larger production volumes, R&D prototypes, rapid prototyping and high-precision component development, while continuing to support customers across India and abroad.

Why Choose MSK?

  • Engineering-led manufacturing: every part is reviewed by design and FEA engineers before machining for manufacturability and performance.
  • Precision CNC machining: tight-tolerance turning, milling, drilling and grinding for aerospace, defence, EV, robotics and industrial components.
  • Quality and documentation: inspection reports, dimensional checks and material traceability available for critical parts.
  • Rapid prototyping & product development: concept to CAD, FEA, machining, finishing and validation under one roof.
  • Multi-industry experience: Defence, Aerospace, Oil & Gas, Marine, Automotive, Rail, Robotics, Agriculture and EV components.

Our Capabilities

We deliver high-precision manufacturing and engineering solutions combining advanced CNC machining, reverse engineering, and rigorous quality control to meet tight tolerances and demanding schedules.

  • Multi-axis CNC turning, milling, drilling and surface grinding
  • Reverse engineering & digitisation from physical parts and legacy spares
  • Materials: carbon steels, stainless steels, aluminium, titanium, brass and specialty alloys
  • Prototype-to-production with inspection reports and documentation
  • Lean–Agile operations for speed, consistency and reliability

Engineering Services

Practical engineering support that connects design decisions with real machining and fabrication constraints. CAD/CAM for seamless handoff to manufacturing.

  • Mechanical Design: 3D modelling, detailing, tolerance stacks and design optimisation.
  • FEA (Finite Element Analysis): static, modal, fatigue, buckling and thermal stress checks.
  • CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics): internal/external flow, pressure drop and heat transfer.
  • DFM (Design for Manufacture): process selection, cost optimisation and yield improvement.
  • Reverse Engineering: 3D scanning/CAD reconstruction for discontinued or undocumented parts.
  • Project Management: scope, schedule, risk, supplier coordination and compliance support.

3D Printing & Additive Manufacturing

Industrial-grade additive manufacturing for rapid prototyping, fixtures, tooling and low-volume production.

  • Technologies: SLA, FDM and metal AM (planned) for engineering applications
  • Applications: brackets, manifolds, jigs & fixtures, housings, guards and concept validation parts
  • Benefits: faster design verification, reduced tooling cost and integration with CNC finishing
  • Support for hybrid manufacturing – combining 3D printing with machining for final accuracy
