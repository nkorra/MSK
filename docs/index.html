About MSK – Our Story

MSK Precision Engineering Group began in 2019 as a small workshop in Cherlapally, Hyderabad, focused on delivering accurate machining and fabrication with clear timelines and straightforward communication.

Today we provide CNC machining, CAM, FEA/CFD, reverse engineering, rapid prototyping, additive manufacturing and digital quality systems for demanding applications. Our goal remains the same – to be a dependable engineering partner who understands both the drawing and the shop floor and treats every part as if it were going into our own project.

Services

Manufacturing Capabilities

  • CNC turning, milling & drilling – prototype to small/medium batch
  • High-precision components for Defence, Aerospace, Marine & EV
  • Jigs, fixtures, tooling and custom adapters
  • Reverse engineering of legacy parts and imported components

Engineering & Design

  • 3D CAD modelling, manufacturing drawings and GD&T
  • Design for Manufacture (DFM) and design optimisation
  • FEA – static, fatigue, buckling and thermal checks
  • CFD – internal/external flow, cooling and pressure-drop studies

Data, Automation & Precision Engineering

Modern manufacturing needs more than machines – it needs connected information. At MSK, we treat data as another engineering asset, just like drawings, load cases and tolerances. We combine shop-floor data, engineering models and digital workflows to make our decisions faster and our processes more stable.

  • CNC machine data, inspection reports and traveller history
  • In-house tracking of revisions, fixtures, programs and delivery milestones
  • Statistical methods & machine learning to spot variation and scrap drivers
  • Automated, repeatable workflows to avoid manual errors

3D Printing & Rapid Prototyping

  • Functional prototypes and fixtures
  • Low-volume covers, housings and brackets
  • Design validation before investing in hard tooling
