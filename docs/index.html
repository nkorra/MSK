About MSK – Our Story

MSK Precision Engineering Group began in 2019 as a small workshop in Cherlapally, Hyderabad, supporting local industries with reliable machining and fabrication. From day one, the focus was simple: accurate parts, honest timelines, and clear technical communication.

Over time, MSK expanded into CAD/CAM, FEA/CFD support, reverse engineering, rapid prototyping, and additive manufacturing. As customer needs grew, so did our scope – from one-off parts to assemblies, fixtures, jigs, prototype components and precision parts for strategic sectors.

Today, MSK is building a new facility at IP-Madharam while continuing to serve Cherlapally and the wider Hyderabad industrial belt. We support Defence, Aerospace, Oil & Gas, Marine & Offshore, Robotics, Automotive, Rail and Agriculture clients with an integrated offering: design review, manufacturability optimisation, CNC machining, 3D printing, inspection, and documentation.

The goal remains the same as in 2019 – to be a dependable engineering partner who understands both the drawing and the shop floor, and who treats every part as if it were going into our own project.

Our Capabilities

We deliver high-precision manufacturing and engineering solutions, combining advanced CNC machining, reverse engineering, and rigorous quality control to meet tight tolerances and fast lead times.

  • Multi-axis CNC turning, milling, drilling, and surface grinding
  • Reverse engineering & digitisation from physical parts and legacy spares
  • Materials: steel, aluminium, titanium, brass, stainless steel, and specialty alloys
  • Prototype-to-production with inspection reports and documentation
  • Lean–Agile operations for speed, consistency, and reliability

Engineering Services

Practical engineering support that connects design decisions with real machining and fabrication constraints. CAD/CAM for seamless handoff to manufacturing.

  • FEA (Finite Element Analysis): static, modal, fatigue, buckling, thermal stress
  • CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics): internal/external flow, pressure drop, heat transfer, aerodynamics
  • DFM (Design for Manufacture): tolerance/fit, process selection, cost and yield optimisation
  • Reverse Engineering: 3D scanning/CAD reconstruction for discontinued, imported, or undocumented parts
  • Project Management: scope, schedule, risk, supplier coordination, and compliance support
  • Lean Methods: 5S, Kaizen, SMED, and standard work to reduce waste and variability

3D Printing Solutions

Industrial-grade additive manufacturing for rapid prototyping, fixtures, tooling and low-volume production.

  • 3D Printers: 1× metal SLM and 1× polymer SLA/FDM
  • Materials: stainless steels and alloys (metal), engineering resins and industrial polymers (SLA/FDM)
  • Applications: brackets, manifolds, jigs & fixtures, housings, guards, and concept validation parts
  • Benefits: accelerated design verification, reduced time-to-market, and integration with CNC finishing
