Our Capabilities

We deliver high-precision manufacturing and engineering solutions, combining advanced CNC machining, additive manufacturing, and rigorous quality control to meet tight tolerances and fast lead times.

  • Multi-axis CNC turning, milling, drilling, and surface grinding
  • Materials: steel, aluminium, titanium, brass, stainless steel, specialty alloys
  • Prototype-to-production with inspection reports and documentation
  • Lean–Agile operations for speed, consistency, and reliability

Engineering Services

End-to-end design and analysis to de-risk your product before it hits the shop floor — integrated with CAD/CAM for seamless handoff to manufacturing.

  • FEA (Finite Element Analysis): static, modal, fatigue, buckling, thermal stress
  • CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics): internal/external flow, pressure drop, heat transfer, aerodynamics
  • DFM (Design for Manufacture): tolerance/fit, process selection, cost and yield optimisation
  • Project Management: scope, schedule, risk, supplier coordination, and compliance support
  • Lean Methods: 5S, Kaizen, SMED, and standard work to reduce waste and variability

3D Printing Solutions

Industrial-grade additive manufacturing for rapid prototyping, fixtures, and low-volume production in metals and polymers.

  • Technologies: FDM, SLA, SLS
  • Materials: PLA, ABS, PETG, Nylon, engineering resins
  • Applications: medical fixtures, aerospace prototypes, tooling and jigs
  • Benefits: accelerated design verification and reduced time-to-market
