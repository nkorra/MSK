About MSK – Our Story

MSK Precision Engineering Group began in 2019 as a small workshop in Cherlapally, Hyderabad, supporting local industries with reliable machining and fabrication. From day one, we focused on doing the basics extremely well: accurate parts, honest timelines, and clear technical communication.

Over time, MSK expanded its capability into CAD/CAM, FEA/CFD support, rapid prototyping, and additive manufacturing. As customer needs grew, so did our scope – from one-off parts to assemblies, fixtures, jigs, prototype components and precision parts for strategic sectors.

In 2025, MSK moved into a modern facility at IP-Madharam, enabling us to bring CNC machining, 3D printing, engineering simulation and R&D support under one roof. Today, we support Defence, Aerospace, Oil & Gas, Marine & Offshore, Robotics, Automotive & EV, Rail, Agriculture, Renewable/Hydro Energy and high-precision manufacturing customers.

Our journey is guided by a simple belief: manufacturing becomes truly powerful when precision, engineering and innovation come together.

Our Capabilities

We deliver high-precision manufacturing and engineering solutions, combining advanced CNC machining, additive manufacturing, and rigorous quality control to meet tight tolerances and fast lead times.

  • Multi-axis CNC turning, milling, drilling, and surface grinding
  • Materials: steel, aluminium, titanium, brass, stainless steel, specialty alloys
  • Prototype-to-production with inspection reports and documentation
  • Lean–Agile operations for speed, consistency, and reliability

Engineering Services

End-to-end design and analysis with CAD/CAM for seamless handoff to manufacturing.

  • FEA (Finite Element Analysis): static, modal, fatigue, buckling, thermal stress
  • CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics): internal/external flow, pressure drop, heat transfer, aerodynamics
  • DFM (Design for Manufacture): tolerance/fit, process selection, cost and yield optimisation
  • Project Management: scope, schedule, risk, supplier coordination, and compliance support
  • Lean Methods: 5S, Kaizen, SMED, and standard work to reduce waste and variability

3D Printing Solutions

Industrial-grade additive manufacturing for rapid prototyping, fixtures, and low-volume production in metals and polymers.

  • Technologies: FDM, SLA, SLS
  • Materials: PLA, ABS, PETG, Nylon, engineering resins
  • Applications: medical fixtures, aerospace prototypes, tooling and jigs, R&D test rigs
  • Benefits: accelerated design verification and reduced time-to-market
