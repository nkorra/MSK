About MSK

About MSK

MSK Precision Engineering Group supports precision manufacturing, CAD/CAM readiness, structural validation and engineering-led manufacturing coordination for marine, offshore, defence, aerospace and industrial applications. MSK also supports propulsion system design, propeller prototyping and validation for marine and unmanned applications.
