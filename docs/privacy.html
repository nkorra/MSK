Privacy Policy

MSK Precision Engineering Group Privacy Policy

MSK Precision Engineering Group respects your privacy. This page explains how we collect and use information submitted through our website, enquiry forms and chatbot.

Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry, quote request, Ask MSK chatbot message or job application, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, company, service requirement, project details and uploaded files such as drawings, specifications or CVs.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information only to respond to enquiries, prepare quotations, review service requirements, communicate about projects, assess job applications and improve our engineering and manufacturing services.

Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and Google Analytics to understand website traffic, see which pages are useful and improve our services. Cookie consent is stored in your browser using localStorage so the notice does not appear repeatedly. Analytics information is used in an aggregated way to improve the website experience.

Files and Technical Data

Drawings, CAD files, RFQs, CVs or supporting documents submitted through the website are used only for enquiry, quotation, application review and related communication.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared internally with MSK team members or trusted service providers only where needed to respond to your enquiry or deliver requested services.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact info@mskprecisiongroup.com.
